Meet KHI

KHI is a fitness gadget that detect the body movements and use the data to process information so the athlete can optimize the routine, amend mistakes, learn how to do the exercise correctly and avoid damage.

KHI alert the user instantly in case he/she needs to correct the exercise, even without a coach. It keep the data to be analysed later by a specialist and to mantain personal statistics about the training.

If you're interested in KHI, help us making it better and answer our survey here!.

Right now, Khi is in the phase of concept testing and prototype iteration.

During the development stage, Khi has the support of the following academic departments: kinesiología and ingeniería en biomedicina of Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile.
